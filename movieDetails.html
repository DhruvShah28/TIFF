Viet and Nam

Trương Minh Quý

NORTH AMERICAN PREMIERE

Philippines, Singapore, France, Netherlands, Italy, Germany, Vietnam | 2024 | 129m | Vietnamese

Amidst the darkness 1,000 metres underground sparkles an intimate romance between Viet and Nam in this mystical story of two young miners in search of a brighter future.

A group of soot-covered men stand chest to chest as they descend on a shabby lift down to near-complete darkness, where they toil away like machines. Though the mine feels like a space of confinement at first, it’s a place of liberty for Viet and Nam. ...

Just before its premiere at Cannes, Viet and Nam was banned by Vietnam’s Cinema Department for its “gloomy, deadlocked, and negative view” of the country...
